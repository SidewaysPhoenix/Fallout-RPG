**Ranks:** 1  

You believe your superiors in the Brotherhood lack a vision beyond acquiring pre-War technology and that your strength as a member of this fraternity of military descendants gives you a duty to help others and pursue different goals than the dogma of the Brotherhood leadership dictates. 

You gain one additional Tag skill, which must be one of Energy Weapons, Science, or Repair. 

You gain an additional 1d20 junk (see **Fallout: The Roleplaying Game Core Rulebook**, p.200) and can spend 1 AP up to 3 times for 1 Uncommon material per AP spent. You can make one additional roll on the loot table without having to spend AP.
