###### RAGE HEAL
**Requirements:** 

**Description:**  
The creature is driven by fury and adrenaline, and needs to be put down quickly or it will shake off any damage it suffers. 

**Effect:**  
The creature regains 3 HP at the start of each of its turns. 

**Mutation:**  
When this creature mutates, it immediately heals back to its maximum HP.
