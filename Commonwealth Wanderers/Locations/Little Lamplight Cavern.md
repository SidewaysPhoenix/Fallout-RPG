# ⚠️ SEGMENT 1 – TOXIC GAS POCKETS

### Setup (What you say)

“The tunnel ahead is low and tight. A greenish haze clings to the ground, shifting slowly. Every few seconds you hear a faint hiss from cracks in the rock and the haze rises quickly and dissipates back to a low fog.”

---

## Mechanics

Due to corrosive fog players entering the deep fog must make:

**END + Survival (or equivalent)**  
Difficulty: 2

---

## Outcomes

### ✔ Success

- They move through safely

### ❌ Fail

- Take **2 damage ignoring armor**
- Gain **+1 difficulty on next physical check**

### 💥 Complication

- Gas surges:
    - Everyone must immediately make a Difficulty 1 check
    - Or take 2 damage

---

## Optional Player Solutions (reduce difficulty to 0)

- Wet clothes over body
- Move quickly between seconds
- Use gear creatively

---

# ⚠️ SEGMENT 2 – PATH CHOICE (REAL DECISION)

You present:

> “The tunnel splits into three routes.”

---

## Path A – Narrow Crawl

### Mechanics

**AGI + Athletics**  
Difficulty: 1

### Fail

- Stuck briefly → need help to be freed

### Complication

- Small collapse → take 2 damage and are stuck

### Benefit

- Skip Segment 3 entirely (this is the “safe” route)

---

## Path B – Open Cavern

### Mechanics

**PER + Survival**  
Difficulty: 2

### Fail

- Falling debris → 3 damage

### Complication

- Lose footing → sprained ankle reducing movement by half until properly healed.

### Benefit

- Find **minor loot stash**  
    (ammo, stim, etc.)

---

## Path C – Old Walkway

### Mechanics

**STR + Athletics**  
Difficulty: 2

### Fail

- Fall → 3 damage

### Complication

- Break section → next player difficulty +1 and fall

### Benefit

- Reach boss faster → get **surprise round**

---
