## Lexington Square

Surrounded by several shopfronts that people of the past would spend there day perusing wares and enjoying a day out
  The middle of the square has a statue dedicated to someone from long ago with a plaque on it. This is a Statue of Captain John Parker
  The surrounding storefronts are
  * Red Rocket Truck Stop
  * Slocum Joes
  * Super Duper Mart
  * Mass Outfitters
  * Lexington Bank
  * Hardware Store
  * Faded Glory Laundromat
  * Parking Garage
    
    Around Lexington square is several hidden Ghouls if the players go to the center first they will all attack at once, if the go store by store they will pop out a little at a time.
    High above on the opposite side of the statue is a raider on a walkway with a suit of power armor, no pieces, and a Fat Man with 1 Mini Nuke.
	    This raider can be detected at day with a perception survival difficulty 3 from the sound of his footsteps during the day.
	    During the night he will be easier to detect with a difficulty of 2 from the lit barrel next to him casting light.
	    To detect that he has a Fat Man will require a Perception Big Guns check of 3, if the players mention the have one so they should detect it easier give that player a temp luck bonus +2.
 

##### Red Rocket Truck Stop

The Red Rocket Truck Stop has 2d6 junk an armor repair bench. Behind it is a power armor repair station.

##### Faded Glory Laundromat

The Laundromat has a small interior with several washing machines
* A perception survival check 2 provide 3 d6 bobby pins and an "Overdue Book", along with a [[First Aid Kit]] in the back
* Along with a cigarette machine in the corner that will give 1 pack of cigarettes for 1 AP

##### Parking Garage
The Parking garage has 3 tiers 
The second tier is the main floor - with a staircase that leads to the down to 1st tier and up to the 3rd (roof). There is also a ramp that normally goes down to the first tier that has been barricaded with wood and steel leaving only a door to go down.
* 1st Tier (Roof) - 1d20 worth of caps can be found amongst the cars
* 2nd Tier nothing of note other than some burnt out cars, a maintenance office with some lockers (1d6 junk) and the staircase and ramp.
* 3rd Tier
  Entering from the stairs - the players will see a terminal (difficulty 1) and a protectron station, along with (Difficutly 2 Perseption Survival) a dead settler, ammo can, and first aid kit in the back of a truck.
  Entering from the ramp - the players will see the path making a u back toward them going down and a few dead ghouls.
  
  In the middle of these points is 6 ghouls all standing on oil, if the players can detect it (Perception + Science difficulty 3) they can ignite it to light up the ghouls.
  After defeating the ghouls there is a locked difficulty 1 cage where someone has stashed a box of chems 1 roll, and an explosives container 2 rolls 3 with an extra AP.

##### Mass Outfitters

Mass Outfitters is just a small building mostly destroyed with 2 mannequins in the window allowing for 2 clothing rolls.

##### Super Duper Mart

Upon entry of the Super Duper Mart you see a Protectron Charging station covered with the dust of time
* The players may just remove the dust to see there is a Protectron inside
  Or they can make a perception survival check difficulty 1 to detect the Protectron inside
* Dead Ghouls litter the floor corpses picked clean with a Y junction in front of them to the left is the checkout area, to the right is the beginning of the store where you would start.
* Not all Ghouls are dead however as many are playing dead, 3 in the first area use the corebook to determine player rolls for this scenario
* In the center of the store the roof has collapsed making traversal difficult
* Towards the back of the store is a locked door difficulty 1 with a dead ghoul and a dead minuteman inside, this looks to be the pharmacy.
  On Entry if the players are not stealthed drops 2 feral ghouls 1 from each overhead half window from the streets. **If all players are stealthed the combat will not initiate.**
  On the Minutemans body is his Militia Hat and 1 roll table of food items and a clothing item. Next to him is his short Laser Musket, 2 Rad X
  Also in this room is 8 Fusion cells and 10 10mm along with an Ammo Can (2 Ammo roll, 3 with 1 AP) in the bathroom.
  Searching the shelves grants 1 Stimpak, 2 Mentats.
* Outside of the pharmacy in the waiting area is a magazine turnstile with the ability to roll on the magazine table. 1 Ap can be spent to find another but no rare books can be obtained here.
* Directly away from the left side of the Y junction past checkout is the Coffee Shop
  Inside here is 2 dead ghouls and 4 playing dead (2 behind the counter, 2 in the dining area)
  On searching a first aid kit can be found here with 1 roll on the chem table, and prewar money in the register.
* Checkout has 3 registers that can be looted for prewar money
* If the players go left instead of right they will find the managers office with a locked terminal, a locked safe both difficulty 1
  Hacking the terminal allows for controlling the Protectron and unlocking the safe
  The safe has 3 rolls of ammunition, 1 d6 junk and a pipe pistol
* Looting the shelves in the main part of the store grants 5 food rolls and players will note the halloween decorations still on shelves, granting 2d6 junk.
* In the back right is the back stocking areas where the players can go left or ahead and sharp right
  Going left is a stock room with a dead feral ghoul , a ghoul playing dead and a ghoul hiding under a shelf.
  On the floor of this room is a flammable liquid, that can be detected with a PER Survival difficulty 2 or Science Difficulty 2.
  In this room is a dead Minuteman and steamer trunk 
  Looting the steamer trunk there is 2d20 caps, 1 ammo roll, one small guns weapon roll, 1 armor roll
  On the minuteman is 5D20 Caps, his short laser musket, his minuteman outfit, Militia hat, Josh's holotape
  The holotape depicts Josh coming back with Emma to discover everything a wreck and a couple of his people gone only for the feral ghouls to attack him as well.
  
  Going straight and through the back tunnels is the generator room with 2 dead and one playing dead ghoul, on the floor of this room is also flammable liquid
  One of the generators still seems to be functioning, humming with life
  Opposite the generators is a set of stairs going down
  Following the right leads to the company break room where if the players are not stealthed 3 ghouls drop from the overhead windows initiating combat.
  In here is a cigarette machine with up to 3 packs of cigarettes 1 by default up to 3 if 2 more ap is spent
  Beyond that is the bathroom with a chem box allowing 1 chem roll
* Downstairs leads to the Garage with closed doors that go outside to the Commonwealth 
  In this room is 2 ghouls playing dead and 1 corpse, along with a Minuteman corpse, a Terminal on the wall, an ammo can, for 1 ammo roll, and 1 explosives crate. And a large object underneath a sheet.
  On the Minuteman corpse is her short laser musket, Minuteman Outfit, Militia Hat, 1 food item and Emma's Holotape 
  On Emma's Holotape you hear is Emma wondering why Josh is taking so long, her hearing gunfire and then screaming out Josh's name.
  The terminal is unlocked and opens the garage door.

##### Slocum Joes

A Slocum Joes, with a diner area, and at the back is a small room behind a door with a sign that says "Employees Only" and a set of stairs going upstairs and downstairs
* In the Main Diner area you find some prewar money, and dirty water
* In the "Employees only room is the kitchen with 2 Nuka Colas and a PreWar food item"
* Downstairs is a prep area with some dirty water and 1d6 common junk 
  On a successful Difficulty 3 Perception Stealth roll you discover an elevator behind a bookcase, with a button next to it.
* Upstairs the roof is caved in with the ability to access the roof with an Athletics 2 difficulty, Complication 19 or higher due to rubble

##### Lexington Bank

Lexington Bank is relatively small with the front wall blown open, there is a countertop with 3 registers that has some prewar money in it and a terminal next to the vault.
* The terminal is unlocked and allows for opening the vault
  On opening the vault door the players see a massive hole in the wall with a skeleton draped over it and the shelves barren except for small amounts of prewar money.
  On the other side of the whole is a 5ft drop with a fallen ladder on the ground, some more loose prewar money, and a dufflebag next to a skeleton with loads of prewar money and a burned out car.|

##### Hardware Store

The hardware store is a fairly small store with blown out walls 
Upon searching the hardware store the players will find:
* A raider corpse with a double-barrel shotgun, 4 shotgun shells and a leather left arm
* An ammo can with 1 roll on the ammunition table allowing for 3 max with AP
* A chem cooler with 1 roll on the chem table
