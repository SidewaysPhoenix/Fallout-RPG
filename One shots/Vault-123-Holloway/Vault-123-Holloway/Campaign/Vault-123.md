# 🏛️ Vault 123 — World Seed

> *"PROJECT HOLLOWAY — Extraction Complete. Personality Matrix Integrity: 98.3%. Awaiting Overseer authorization for matrix deployment."*

## What Is Vault 123?

Vault 123 is introduced at the **end of The Swamp Remembers** as the true reality behind the one-shot. The entire Point Lookout scenario was a **Personality Matrix Extraction** — a constructed psychological immersion designed to fully map, record, and archive each subject's complete personality while they believed they were living it.

The players' characters are not wastelanders. They are **pre-war or early-war civilians** who entered Vault 123 voluntarily or otherwise, were processed through Project Holloway, and sealed into cryogenic stasis. They have been on ice ever since.

**They were not tested. They were opened up and copied.**

## The Experiment — PROJECT HOLLOWAY

Named after **Dr. Lenora Holloway**, a pre-war psychologist who theorized that personality could only be fully revealed — never self-reported — through high-stakes narrative immersion. Vault-Tec acquired her research in 2067 and weaponized it.

The process:
1. Subject enters stasis
2. A constructed scenario is fed directly into the subject's unconscious mind via neural interface — immersive, indistinguishable from reality
3. The subject's every decision, emotional response, and moral choice is recorded
4. Upon scenario completion, a full **Personality Matrix** is compiled and archived
5. Subject remains in stasis, unaware the extraction occurred

The Point Lookout scenario was chosen specifically because it forces subjects to confront **violence, mercy, inherited trauma, and moral ambiguity** — the full spectrum Holloway's model required.

## What This Leaves Open for the GM

- What is **matrix deployment**? RoboBrains? Synth precursors? A Vault-Tec AI? A weapon?
- Who is the Overseer, and why are they offline?
- Where are the archived matrices stored — and are they still intact?
- Where is Vault 123 located in the campaign world?
- When (if ever) do the characters wake up?
- If their personalities were copied — what is *running* them now?
