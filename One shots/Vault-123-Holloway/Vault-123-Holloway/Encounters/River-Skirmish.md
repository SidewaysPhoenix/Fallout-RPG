# ⚔️ River Skirmish

## Purpose

A short warmup encounter to get dice on the table, establish the 2d20 combat rhythm, and set the tone before the swamp. Not meant to be lethal or draining.

---

## Setup

Mid-crossing, the Duchess Gambit is approached by either:

**Option A — Bloatfly Swarm**
- 6–8 [[Bloatfly]], basic stats from core book
- Fast, annoying, spread across the deck
- Good for teaching action economy

**Option B — Raider Skiff**
- 2–3 [[Raider]] on a cobbled-together raft
- They want the boat. They are bad at tactics.
- Slightly more interesting roleplay (they can be scared off without killing)

*Choose based on your players. Combat-focused group: Raiders. New players: Bloatflies (simpler targets).*

---

## Terrain

The ferry deck is **narrow** — limited cover, moving water below. Falling overboard costs 1 turn to climb back aboard and soaks ammunition (GM discretion on damage).

---

## Resolution

1–2 rounds. End it quickly. This is not the main event.

**If players are struggling:** Tobar appears with a shotgun and an annoyed expression. He helps. He hates that he helped.
