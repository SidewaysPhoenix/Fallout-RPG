# ⚔️ Cathedral Final Encounter

## Setup

**Marcus** is bound to the stone altar below. **[[Brother-Elias|Brother Elias]]** stands on a raised platform with **3 Tribal Zealots** flanking the walls.

Elias does **not attack first.** He speaks. Allow this. The moral weight of the session lands here.

---

| Enemy                        | Count | Position                      |
| ---------------------------- | ----- | ----------------------------- |
| Brother Elias[[Raider Boss]] | 1     | Raised platform (high ground) |
| Tribal Zealots[[Raider]]     | 3     | Wall positions (flanking)     |

---

## Battlefield

| Feature | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Raised platform (Elias) | High ground — +1 die on ranged attacks, -1 die against melee |
| Stone pillars | Cover (DR 4), scattered throughout the nave |
| Altar (Marcus) | Players must protect Marcus — if he takes damage, he's incapacitated further |
| Bell tower ropes | Can be cut to drop the bell — improvised area attack, GM discretion |

---

## Elias's Special Ability

**Zealot's Ward:** Once per combat, when Elias would be taken out, he spends 1 AP to stay standing at 1 HP. All 3 Zealots immediately take a free action.

---

## Resolution Paths

### Path 1 — Fight
Standard combat. Defeat Elias and the Zealots.

- Elias uses his Tribal Axe in melee and throws chem grenades (Area 2, Radiation 1) at groups
- Zealots use basic Tribal Raider attacks
- When Elias hits 0 HP (after Zealot's Ward triggers), he kneels rather than begs

### Path 2 — Negotiate
**Speech TN 15.** Convince Elias that Marcus is unclean for the ritual.

Acceptable arguments:
- Marcus is diseased (radiation sickness, mutation)
- His bloodline is wrong ("tainted by the old world machines")
- The spirits themselves rejected him (roleplay it — Elias wants to believe this)

**On success:** Elias releases Marcus. Players must leave Point Lookout permanently (in the fiction of the VR — this has no real consequence after the twist).

### Path 3 — Expose the Lie
**Requires [[Elias-Journal|Elias's Journal]] found in the Vestry.**

Show it to the Zealots and reveal that Elias fabricated parts of the doctrine. The Zealots hesitate, then stand down. Elias fights alone at **-2 difficulty** (reduced stats, demoralized).

---

## After Combat

Marcus is alive. Drugged. Cannot walk unaided.

One player must support him throughout the exit. This is intentional — it slows the pace and adds physical weight to the victory.

On the ferry, quietly, Marcus says:
> *"They sang to me every night. Not to scare me. Just... hymns. Old ones. From before."*

**Leave this unresolved.** The ending sequence follows.
