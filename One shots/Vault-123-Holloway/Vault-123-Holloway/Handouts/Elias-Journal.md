# 📖 Brother Elias's Journal

*A worn leather journal, pages bloated with swamp damp. The handwriting is careful, deliberate — this is a man who thinks before he writes.*

---

## Selected Entries

---

**Entry — Earliest (undated)**

> *I dreamed of a place before this. Clean walls. Cold air. A woman in a white coat who told me to relax, that everything would feel very natural. A hum, low and constant, like the world breathing.*
>
> *I don't know what it means. The swamp does strange things to memory. I have stopped trying to hold onto what came before.*

---

**Entry — Undated**

> *The land does not forgive. That is what they do not understand in the cities, in their metal towers. They think distance protects them. It does not. The sickness is in the soil. In the water. In the blood of everyone who breathes this air.*
>
> *We are all carriers.*

---

**Entry — Undated**

> *I did not choose this. The doctrine chose me. I read what was left in the Cathedral — the old writings, the pre-war sermons — and I understood what the founders understood. Sacrifice is not cruelty. It is transaction. You give something precious to receive something necessary.*
>
> *The land is tired. We are asking it to keep giving.*

---

**Entry — Recent**

> *They call me a monster. But I have seen what the old world left behind. I am trying to heal it. The land is sick. We are all sick. The sacrifice is not cruelty — it is medicine. It is the only medicine left.*

---

**Entry — Most Recent**

> *The vessel is strong. Clean-world born. I think he is afraid. I told Brother Cain to sing to him at night — the old hymns. Fear is not something I want to give him.*
>
> *I am sorry for what comes next. I am not sorry for why.*

---

*The journal ends here. Several later pages have been torn out.*

---

## GM Notes

### The Empathy Beat
This handout reframes Elias from villain to tragedy before the final confrontation. Show it to players in the Vestry. Every resolution path is more meaningful when they understand he is broken, not evil.

It also serves as the **evidence for the "Expose the Lie" resolution path** — his own admissions can turn the Zealots against him.

### The Bleed-Through (Project Holloway)
The **first entry** is new — a fragment from the real world bleeding into the simulation. Elias doesn't know what it means. The players won't either, until the ending.

The woman in the white coat is **Dr. Lenora Holloway**, or one of her technicians. The hum is the stasis pod. The "clean walls" are Vault 123.

The extraction wasn't perfectly sealed. Something crossed over. This is a quiet, deniable detail — but on reflection after the reveal, it recontextualizes Elias as a character built from genuine psychological material rather than pure fiction. The scenario pulled from *somewhere.*

Whether that has deeper implications is entirely your GM's call.
