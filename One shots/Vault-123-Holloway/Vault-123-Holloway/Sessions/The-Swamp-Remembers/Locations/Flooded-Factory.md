# 🏭 The Flooded Factory

## Description

A pre-war chemical plant half-submerged in brackish swamp water. The Tribals have set up a forward camp in the upper levels — dry enough to sleep in, high enough to watch the surrounding marsh.

**Atmosphere:**
> *Catwalks groan overhead. The water below is an unsettling greenish-yellow — whatever was stored here didn't go away, it just changed. Something floats near the far wall. You don't look too long.*

---

## Environmental Hazard

**Radioactive Runoff:** Any character standing in the flooded lower level takes **1 Rad per turn** until they climb out. Rads are removed normally after the encounter.
