# 👩 Eliza Crane

## Profile

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Role** | Rivet City merchant, quest giver |
| **Personality** | Practical, worried, trying not to show how scared she is |
| **Motivation** | Get her brother back. That's all. |
| **Reward Offered** | 500 caps per player + covered passage |

## Description

> *A woman who looks like she hasn't slept in a week and is pretending she has. She approaches you directly, no preamble, and lays out the situation like a business transaction because that's the only way she knows how to hold it together.*

---

## Opening Pitch

> *"My brother took Tobar's ferry to Point Lookout fourteen days ago. He was supposed to be back in three. I'm not asking you to be heroes. I'm asking you to find out what happened and bring him home. Five hundred caps each. Tobar covers the passage."*

---

## After the Session

If Marcus is returned safely: Eliza pays in full, no argument. She holds it together until Marcus is off the ferry, then quietly cries where she thinks no one is watching.

If players expose Tobar: She redirects her grief into anger at Tobar — a cleaner emotion. She'll push for consequences.

---

## Note

Eliza is a simulation character. After the VR twist, she does not exist outside the orientation sequence. The GM may choose to never address this, or may one day have a waking player ask about her — and find nothing.
