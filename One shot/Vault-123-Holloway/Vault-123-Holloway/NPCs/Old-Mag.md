# 🐟 Old Mag

## Profile

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Role** | Swamp town information broker |
| **Personality** | Cryptic, trades in favors, smells like catfish |
| **Motivation** | Survival. She knows everything because knowing things keeps her alive. |

## Description

> *An old woman with skin like dried leather and eyes like a catfish — flat, patient, missing nothing. She's sitting outside her shack mending a net when you find her. She doesn't stop mending.*

---

## What She Knows

- Brother Elias has been "calling the blood moon" and requires "clean-world flesh" for the ritual
- The Cathedral's location — will mark it on a map
- That outsiders have been going missing for weeks
- Vaguely, that the Cathedral is pre-war — something was wrong with that ground long before the tribals

## Interaction

**Base attitude:** Neutral. She'll talk, but not for free.

**Price for information:**
- 50 caps, OR
- Successful Barter TN 10, OR
- A good story (successful Speech TN 9 — "entertain me")

**What she says about the Cathedral:**
> *"North. Follow the bells at dusk — they ring every night, regular as a clock. Those cathedral folk, they didn't come from here. Place was like that long before the tribals. Something wrong about that ground. Always was."*

**If asked about Elias directly:**
> *"True believer. Worst kind. A man who does terrible things and sleeps well after."*

---

## Stats

Non-combatant. If threatened, she disappears into the swamp with unnerving speed. Do not stat her for combat.
