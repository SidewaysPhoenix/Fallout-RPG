# ✝️ Brother Elias

## Profile

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Role** | High Priest of the Ark & Dove Tribals |
| **Personality** | True believer — not cartoonishly evil, genuinely broken |
| **Motivation** | He believes the ritual will heal the wasteland. He is wrong. He does not know he is wrong. |

## Description

> *A tall man, lean from swamp living, in robes that were once pre-war vestments and are now something else entirely. He does not look like a monster. He looks like a man who has been carrying something very heavy for a very long time and has decided it was worth it.*

---

## Characterization

**Elias is not the villain of this story. He is its tragedy.**

He inherited a doctrine that was already broken, added his own sincere belief to it, and became something that does terrible things in service of something he thinks is good. The players should feel the weight of that before they fight or negotiate with him.

**Do not pre-script his monologue.** Improvise from his journal:
- The wasteland is sick because of the old world
- Outsiders carry that sickness
- The ritual is medicine, not murder
- He grieves the necessity of it

**Opening line when players enter the Bell Tower:**
> *"You came a long way. For what? To drag him back to a dying world?"*

---

## Resolution Paths

| Resolution | Method | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| **Fight** | Defeat in combat | See stat block below |
| **Negotiate** | Speech TN 15 | Convince him Marcus is "unclean" — diseased, mutated, wrong bloodline |
| **Expose the Lie** | Present his journal to the Zealots | Reveals he fabricated parts of the doctrine — Zealots stand down, Elias fights alone at -2 difficulty |

**Skills:** Melee 3, Speech 4, Survival 3, Medicine 2

**Equipment:**
- Tribal Axe (Melee, 2d6+3 damage, Vicious 1)
- Shaman's Bag — 3 improvised chem grenades (Area 2, Radiation 1, single use each)
- Tattered robes (no armor bonus, but unsettling)

**Special Ability — Zealot's Ward:** Once per combat, when Elias would be taken out, he spends 1 AP to stay standing at 1 HP with a rallying cry. The Zealots all act immediately.

**Flanked by:** 3 Tribal Zealots (standard Tribal Raider profile)

---

## After Defeat / Resolution

If killed: the Zealots scatter into the swamp. The Cathedral falls silent.

If negotiated with: Elias watches you go with something that might be relief. He doesn't pursue.

If exposed: He fights alone, diminished. If reduced to 0 HP, he does not beg — he kneels and waits. What happens next is the players' choice.
