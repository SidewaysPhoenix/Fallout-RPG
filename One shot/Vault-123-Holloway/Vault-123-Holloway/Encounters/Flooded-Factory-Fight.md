---

## Battlefield

The factory has **two levels:**

- **Lower level** — flooded, radioactive (1 Rad/turn while standing in water)
- **Upper level (catwalks)** — dry, rickety, high ground advantage for the Shaman

The Shaman will **never come down voluntarily.** He throws Molotovs from the catwalks and uses stimpaks on himself. Players need to either climb up to him or shoot him off the platform.

---

## Terrain Features

| Feature | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Radioactive water (lower level) | 1 Rad per turn standing in it |
| Catwalk (upper level) | High ground — +1 die on ranged attacks from up here |
| Rusted shelving | Cover (Damage Resistance 2) but collapses after 3 hits |
| Flooded machinery | Difficult terrain in lower level |

---

## Tactics

- **Raiders** spread out and charge. They're not smart but they're aggressive.
- **Shaman** throws Molotovs at clustered players, uses a chem (Buffout) if engaged in melee.
- If 3+ Raiders are downed, the last one may attempt to flee — players can let him go or pursue.
