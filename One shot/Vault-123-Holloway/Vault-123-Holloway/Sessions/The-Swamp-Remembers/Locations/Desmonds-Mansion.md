# 🏚️ Desmond's Mansion

## Description

A crumbling antebellum manor half-reclaimed by the swamp. From outside it looks abandoned. Inside, Desmond has made it surprisingly liveable — pre-war furniture, a working terminal, shelves of salvaged books, and a fine collection of old world liquor he'll share if he likes you.

**Atmosphere:**
> *The house shouldn't still be standing. But it is — stubbornly, defiantly. Inside smells like old paper and pine resin. Candles burn on a mahogany table covered in maps. A ghoul in a silk waistcoat looks up from his chair.*
>
> *"You found it. Most people can't. Sit down. Don't touch anything."*

---

## What Desmond Provides

### Resupply
- Remove **1 Injury** per character (rest + basic medical supplies)
- Restock minor ammo — up to 20 rounds of common calibers per character
- Optional: a drink that removes 1 point of stress (house rule or flavor only)

### Intel — Cathedral Layout
Desmond has been watching the Cathedral for months. He provides:
- **General layout** — Nave, Vestry, Bell Tower
- **Guard positions** and rough patrol timing
- The **flooded crypt entrance** — a secret way in that bypasses the main gate entirely

> *"There's a crypt on the east side, flooded to the knee. The tribals don't use it — superstitious about the old graves. You can get in through there without them knowing you're coming. Probably."*

### The Moral Seed
Before they leave, Desmond says this — don't skip it:

> *"The tribals didn't start this. A pre-war cult built that cathedral. The tribals just inherited the madness. Doesn't make them less dangerous. Just makes them... sadder."*
