# 🏚️ Pilgrim's Landing

## Description

A small shanty town of swamp folk clinging to the edge of the marsh. Half-feral, deeply suspicious of outsiders. Buildings are a patchwork of salvaged pre-war materials and lashed-together driftwood. Fires burn low. Eyes follow you from doorways.

**Atmosphere:**
> *The town smells like fish and old smoke. People watch you from doorways without moving — not hostile, just... measuring. A dog barks once and goes quiet. Someone hangs a hex sign above their door when they see you coming.*

---

## Key NPC

### [[../../NPCs/Old-Mag|Old Mag]]
A swamp woman who trades in secrets. Will talk to outsiders for the right price — caps, food, or just a good story.

**What she knows:**
- The Tribal high priest — **Brother Elias** — has been "calling the blood moon"
- He needs "clean-world flesh" for the ritual
- She knows where the Cathedral is and will mark it on a map for 50 caps or a successful Barter TN 10

**What she'll say unprompted:**
> *"Those cathedral folk, they didn't come from here. Place was like that long before the tribals. Something wrong about that ground. Always was."*

## Skill Tests

| Test | TN | Result |
|------|----|--------|
| Speech / Barter to get locals talking | 10 | They open up; point to Old Mag |
| Failure on Speech | — | Hostility (not combat — just closed doors) |
| Perception to spot Tribal scout watching town | 11 | Can attempt to capture for patrol route intel |
| Intimidation on captured scout | 10 | He gives up the patrol route |

---

## Tribal Scout

A single Tribal scout is watching the town from the treeline. If spotted and captured:
- Knows the Cathedral's patrol schedule
- Grants **+1d20 advantage** on Sneak tests approaching the Cathedral
- Stats: Basic Tribal Raider (see Modiphius Fallout core book)

## Exits

- Swamp trails lead toward [[Flooded-Factory|The Flooded Factory]] (east)
- A longer trail, marked by cairns, leads to [[Ark-And-Dove-Cathedral|The Cathedral]] (north, once revealed by Old Mag)
- [[Desmonds-Mansion|Desmond's Mansion]] is findable from here with a Survival TN 12 or Desmond's earlier hint
