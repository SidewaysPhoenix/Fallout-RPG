# 🌿 Act 2 — Into the Swamp

## Summary

Three locations that players can tackle in **any order**. Each serves a different party role — investigation, combat, and roleplay/resupply. All three feed into Act 3.

---

## Locations

| Location | Primary Mode | Key Outcome |
|----------|-------------|-------------|
| [[../Locations/Pilgrims-Landing\|Pilgrim's Landing]] | Investigation / Social | Learn about Elias and the Cathedral location |
| [[../Locations/Flooded-Factory\|The Flooded Factory]] | Combat | Find proof Marcus is alive, collect loot |
| [[../Locations/Desmonds-Mansion\|Desmond's Mansion]] | Roleplay / Resupply | Get Cathedral layout + crypt entrance intel |

---

## Pacing Notes

- Players don't *need* all three locations — Pilgrim's Landing and one other is enough to proceed
- If time is running short, skip the Factory and have Desmond provide a condensed version of its intel
- The **moral seed** — that the tribals inherited their madness, didn't create it — should land before Act 3. Desmond delivers it best.

**Desmond's Line (plant this before the Cathedral):**
> *"The tribals didn't start this. A pre-war cult built that cathedral. The tribals just inherited the madness. Doesn't make them less dangerous. Just makes them... sadder."*

---

## Tribal Scout Opportunity

If players capture the scout spotted at Pilgrim's Landing:
- He knows the Cathedral patrol route
- Grants **+1d20 advantage** on Sneak tests when approaching the Cathedral in Act 3
- He breaks under any Intimidation test (TN 10) — he's scared, not loyal

---

## Act Transitions

Once players have:
- ✅ Located the Cathedral (via Old Mag or the scout)
- ✅ Confirmed Marcus is alive (journal page from Factory or Old Mag's intel)

They are ready for [[Act-3-The-Cathedral|Act 3]].
