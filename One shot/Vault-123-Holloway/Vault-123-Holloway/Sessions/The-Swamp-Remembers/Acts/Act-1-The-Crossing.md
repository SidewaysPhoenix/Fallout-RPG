# ⚓ Act 1 — The Crossing

## Summary

The players board Tobar's ferry at Rivet City docks. During the crossing they encounter their first social challenge, a quick combat warmup, and arrive at Point Lookout where [[../../NPCs/Desmond-Lockheart|Desmond Lockheart]] delivers the session's second hook.

---

## Scene 1 — Rivet City Docks

**Read Aloud:**
> *The docks smell like rust and salt water. Tobar's ferry — the Duchess Gambit — bobs lazily against the pier. The man himself is leaning against a post, picking his teeth with a knife. He doesn't look like trouble. He looks like a man who's very good at pretending he isn't.*

### Social Encounter: Tobar

[[../../NPCs/Tobar|Tobar]] is shifty and deflects questions about missing passengers.

- **Persuasion or Insight test — TN 12**
  - **Success:** He's nervous, not just greedy. He *knows* something happened to prior passengers but is too scared to say.
  - **Failure:** He brushes it off. Players may try again later on the water.

> *If pressed hard enough, Tobar mutters: "Point Lookout ain't like other places. Things go missing there. Always have."*

---

## Scene 2 — Mid-River: Bloatfly Swarm / Raider Skiff

See [[../../Encounters/River-Skirmish|River Skirmish]] for full encounter details.

This is a **short warmup encounter** — 1–2 rounds, not lethal. Purpose is to get dice on the table and establish the system rhythm before the swamp.

---

## Scene 3 — Point Lookout Pier

**Read Aloud:**
> *The Duchess Gambit slides into the pier with a groan. The smell hits first — rot, river mud, something sweet and wrong underneath it all. The fog is so thick you can barely see the shore beyond the dock. And leaning against a post, arms crossed, watching you with the patience of someone who's been alive far too long — a ghoul in a fine coat.*

### Meeting Desmond

[[../../NPCs/Desmond-Lockheart|Desmond Lockheart]] has been watching for useful outsiders.

**Desmond's Opening Line:**
> *"Don't get comfortable. This is my swamp, my problems, and I don't need — actually, you know what, maybe I do. How do you feel about cultists?"*

### Desmond's Hook

He tells them:
- Tribal activity near the **Ark & Dove Cathedral** has increased sharply
- He has a **safehouse** they can use if things go sideways (won't say it's his mansion yet)
- He offers a **unique pre-war item** as payment if they deal with the cult problem

> Desmond does not tell them about the flooded crypt entrance yet — save that for [[../Locations/Desmonds-Mansion|his mansion]] later.

---

## Act Transitions

- Players may head to any of the three Act 2 locations in **any order**
- Point toward [[../Locations/Pilgrims-Landing|Pilgrim's Landing]] as the natural first stop — locals, information, and a lead on the Cathedral
