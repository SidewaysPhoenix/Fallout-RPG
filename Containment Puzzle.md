## Room Description

The blast door ahead is sealed shut by a massive hydraulic lock.

A flickering terminal beside it reads:

> **SUBJECT UNSTABLE**  
> **STABILIZATION REQUIRED**  

At the center of the room is a restrained person suspended in a containment tank.

It twitches violently.

Veins bulge beneath stretched skin.

Bone protrusions flex against restraints.

Four separate control consoles surround the chamber.

Each controls one system:

---

### Console 1 — Temperature

- 20°C
- 40°C
- 60°C
- 80°C

---

### Console 2 — Pressure

- 30 PSI
- 45 PSI
- 60 PSI
- 90 PSI

---

### Console 3 — Chemical Feed

- FEV-A
- FEV-B
- FEV-C
- FEV-D

---

### Console 4 — Sedation

- None
- Low
- Moderate
- Heavy

---


# CLUES

These are scattered around the room.

Players can find them naturally by searching.

---

## Clue 1 — Thermal Pressure Relationship

Found on a stained whiteboard:

> Increased chamber heat requires elevated pressure to maintain tissue cohesion.  
> At low heat, excessive pressure causes vascular tearing.

---

## Clue 2 — Chemical Compatibility Chart

Found on a terminal:

> Compound B loses efficacy under temperatures above 40°C.  
> Compound C destabilizes below 60°C.

---

## Clue 3 — Sedation Dependency

Found on a medical clipboard:

> Subjects under low sedation resisted FEV absorption unless chamber pressure exceeded baseline.

(Baseline = 45 PSI)

---

## Clue 4 — Mutation Growth Report

Found beside the subject’s harness:

> At pressures above 60 PSI, subjects exposed to Compound B displayed catastrophic muscular overgrowth.

---

## Clue 5 — Neural Preservation Study

Found in a surgical tray log:

> Moderate sedation preserved neural pathways, but only in subjects receiving Compound D.

---

## Clue 6 — Final Lab Note

Scratched into the wall:

> Forty degrees kept the last viable subject stable long enough to finish transfer.

---

# HOW IT SOLVES

Players must connect:

---

### From Clue 2:

B must be ≤ 40°C

---

### From Clue 6:

40°C worked before

Best fit:  
**40°C**

---

### From Clue 3:

Low sedation requires pressure >45 PSI

So:  
60 or 90

---

### From Clue 4:

B cannot exceed 60 PSI

So:  
**60 PSI**

---

### Clue 5:

Moderate sedation only works with D

But D is a false branch.

So:  
**Low sedation**

---

### Chemical:

B fits all linked requirements.

So:  
**FEV-B**

---



# FAIL STATES

If they lock in a wrong full combination:

Roll 1d2.

---

## 1 — Containment Feedback

Steam erupts from wall vents.

All creatures in room:

AGI / DEX save.

Fail:

Take damage.

Subject grows more unstable.

---

## 2 — Mutation Surge

The subject mutates violently.

Bones snap.

Restraints fail.

It transforms into a Behemoth.

Combat begins.

Blast door remains locked until it dies.

- Subject convulsing
- Restraints groaning
- Blood vessels bursting

Build tension.
