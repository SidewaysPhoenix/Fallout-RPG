###### TYRANT
**Requirements:** 

**Description:**  
More severe than a boss, chief, or alpha, this creature utterly dominates others of its kind.

**Effect:**  
The creature is accompanied by one or more Normal creatures of the same type. There is one extra creature if it is the same level as the Legendary Creature, two extra creatures if they are one or two levels lower, or three extra creatures if they are three or more levels lower.

**Mutation:**  
When this creature mutates, additional creatures arrive as reinforcements; the type and number of reinforcements is the same as the number of extra creatures noted above.
